Potato Recipe

Now we are going to make potato recipe

Ingredients

  • 1/2 kg Potato
  • 200 grm onion
  • 200 grm tomato
  • 01 small size Adrak
  • 50 grm dhniya
  • 1 & half spoon salt
  • 250 grm oil

Method

  1. first of all wash the potato roperly
  2. cut the potato in medium pieces
  3. then cut the oniion, tomato dhniya & adrak in small pieces or grind it,
  4. keep the frypan on the burner and put the oil in it nd keep it fir 3 minutes
  5. then put the paste of onion, tomato etc then mix it with oil
  6. put the potato in it and mix it with paste properly
  7. put 1 x cup of water in it
  8. keep the frypnon on burner for 10 minuts
  9. Take the frypan from burner
  10. Hello my dear, recipe is ready for eating
